MISSION STATEMENT

To usher in a New Era of News Media where traditional journalism intersects with an Urban Hip-Hop flavor.

Podcast

Podcasting is one of the fastest growing media platforms today and the foundation of The Soapboxx. We wanted to create media space where politicians, activists, legal experts, comedians, & more can speak unapolgetic truth on trending topics and news stories.S

The Soapboxx will be produce print and digital content for our audience. As a platform, we are committed to the artform of journalism to inform and empower the public.
