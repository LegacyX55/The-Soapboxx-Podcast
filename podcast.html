Topics

No topic is off limits for The Soapboxx Podcast. We take pride in the versatility of our hosts and guest hosts to give insight on a multitude of subject matters. The following are just a few topics our podcast regularly cover:

News/Politics

We want our audience to stay socially and politically engaged in their respective community. Listen as we interview elected officials, activists, and community leaders. Find out everything you need to know on the latest stories.

Entertainment

Whether it's the newest movie trailer, an album release, or celebrity gossip. Listen as we let you know "what's poppin" in Hollywood and within the culture of Hip-Hop.

Mental Health Awareness

Our podcast is committed to promoting self love and self care. One of the social issues we will continuously advocate for and provide a platform to is mental health.
